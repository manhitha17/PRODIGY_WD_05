About This App

Want to know if it’s sunny or stormy out there? This app gives you live weather updates!!!

🌦️ Tips for Best Use

  • 🔍 Use city names accurately for better results (e.g., “London, UK” or “Paris, France”).
  • 📍 Allow location access to get weather data for your current place instantly.
  • 🌐 Ensure you have a stable internet connection while using the app.
  • 🔄 Refresh the page if results take too long to load.
  • 🧊 For the best visual experience, use the app in fullscreen mode on modern browsers.

We hope this app helps you plan your day better — rain or shine! ☀️☔
